This is a minimalist incremental/idle/puzzle game where you control a spaceship, it's a red square, no it's YOUR red square.

You move around with the arrowkey, you can use your mouse over the map to know what tile is what.

Winning a boss fight will transport you to the next zone, so pick up all powerup before!

I wont tell you too much, the fun is in discovering things but a little hint is to not fight common enemies before zone 5

Dying is permanent, it forces you to prestige and you only get something if you prestige >= zone 8 try to reach that

I think you are ready captain, hop into the map tab and have fun, don't forget to export your savetext for safety
